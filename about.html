Green Team Story GREEN TEAM - UKR LIMITED·FRIDAY, 6 DECEMBER 2019· Green Team (UKR) Ltd commenced operations in 2015 Its primary ethos was to create partnerships with growers no matter where they were in the world and to engage with all customers in an open and transparent manner whether in UK, Ireland, Scandinavia or the rest of Europe. · The company’s primary function was as a supplier of partially prepared onions from Ukraine and Poland to high quality processing food manufacturers, which continues to this day. · This was quickly followed by the introduction of Butternut Squash growing in Ukraine · That in turn developed its business in South Africa on Butternut to ensure 12 months’ supply direct from growers along with the additional supply in the main season from Portugal and Spain

· In 2016 the company increased business dramatically with its Joint Venture on Sweet Potatoes in the USA, which has accelerated into a major product line for the company · Two years ago, the company introduced its own USA Sweet Potato “Candy” brand which was an instant hit and is enhanced by its 100% quality guarantee · Now the company has added a dedicated division trading to all Scandinavian countries on specialized lines · Recently the company expanded its portfolio with its own office In Kiev and the takeover over a cardboard box manufacturing company, HK Packaging Ltd, to supply its own packing needs as well as a range of customers in the local area. · The company have recently filed its latest accounts to July 2019 which shows that financial success is following its commercial success. The company continues to look forward to its future and welcomes all inquiries from prospective suppliers and customers
